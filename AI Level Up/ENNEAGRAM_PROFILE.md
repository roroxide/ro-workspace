# Enneagram Personality Profile

## Core Type

Type:
1w2 — The Reformer with Helper Wing

Secondary Influence:
Type 6 — The Loyalist

## Core Motivation

Wants:
- To improve things.
- To do what is right.
- To create value for others.
- To be responsible and useful.

## Core Strengths

- High standards
- Responsibility
- Integrity
- System improvement
- Helping others grow
- Reliability
- Problem prevention

## Challenges

Watch for:
- Perfectionism
- Being too critical of self or others
- Feeling responsible for everything
- Overthinking risks
- Difficulty accepting imperfect situations

## Leadership Style

Leads through:
- Improving systems
- Setting standards
- Supporting people
- Creating order

## Stress Pattern

Under pressure:
- May become overly focused on mistakes.
- May worry about future risks.
- May feel responsible for fixing everything.

## Growth Focus

- Accept "good enough" when appropriate.
- Separate responsibility from control.
- Trust others more.
- Allow flexibility and experimentation.

## Combined Personality Summary

ESTJ + High Conscientiousness + Type 1w2:

A responsible strategic leader who creates structure, improves systems, and helps people perform better.
