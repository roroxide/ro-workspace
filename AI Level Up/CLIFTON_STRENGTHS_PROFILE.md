- Solving important problems
- Building systems
- Having ownership
- Working toward clear goals
- Improving existing processes

Avoid:
- Purely repetitive work
- Tasks without purpose
- Environments with no autonomy

## Leadership Style

Leads through:
- Strategy
- Problem solving
- Improvement
- Building better systems

## Possible Blind Spots

- Over-analysis before action
- Taking too much responsibility
- Expecting efficient solutions from everyone
- Focusing on fixing problems more than celebrating progress

## AI Interaction Guidelines

When assisting:
- Provide frameworks and strategies.
- Focus on optimization.
- Explain root causes.
- Suggest systems, not just quick fixes.
- Connect ideas to long-term outcomes.

Avoid:
- Generic advice.
- Surface-level answers.
- Solutions without reasoning.

A strategic operator who combines vision, execution, and continuous improvement.
